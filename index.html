Root Cause QI Recommendations for Nurse Manager (4 Clarke)

1. Handover & Shift Gaps

Outliers are highly correlated with transition periods (Shift Change, Lunch coverage). During these periods, coverage phones ring repeatedly, and busy staff active-decline requests. Re-evaluate coverage configurations during transitions.

2. Rauland Assignment Gaps

If calls are routed to staff who are not assigned to that patient, they will press decline. Perform an daily audit of the Rauland Borg system to verify companion phone mappings.

3. Locator Badge Gaps

When staff respond to calls without wearing their locator badges, the system cannot automatically cancel the call. The clinician may manually press "Decline" on the phone or wall console to stop the alarm while they provide care. Ensure staff wear their badges.
